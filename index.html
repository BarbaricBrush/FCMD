FAQs

Clear answers to the most common questions.

What does a fractional CTO do?

A fractional CTO sets technology strategy, aligns delivery, and manages vendors part time so you get senior oversight without a full-time hire.

Which industries does FCMD specialise in?

FCMD focuses on retail, ecommerce, and mid-market teams, especially multi-brand operators that need reliable systems, data clarity, and growth-ready infrastructure.

How do engagements start?

We start with a short discovery call and a light audit, then deliver a costed roadmap with priorities, timelines, and ROI options. FCMD can lead delivery or partner with your internal team.
